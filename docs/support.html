Sunny Support

Need help with Sunny? We are here to help you get your wake-up routine working smoothly.

Contact

Email navyarawal@ucla.edu for support, bug reports, or questions.

Helpful Details to Include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Any screenshots that show the issue

Alarm and Notification Help

If alarms or alerts are not appearing, check that notifications are enabled for Sunny in the iPhone Settings app. Also make sure your alarm is turned on inside Sunny.

Sticker Setup Help

If check-ins are not completing, open Sunny and set up Sunny's spot again. Make sure you bring your phone close to the saved spot when the app asks you to check in.
