Sunny alarm sounds
==================

Bundled wake-up sounds are WAV files because both AVAudioPlayer and iOS local
notification custom sounds handle them reliably.

License-safe sources used for the replacement alarm set:

- rise_classic.wav: "Annoying Alarm Clock" by UncleKornicob, SoundBible, Public Domain.
  Source: https://soundbible.com/1787-Annoying-Alarm-Clock.html
- rise_urgent.wav: "Siren Noise" by KevanGC, SoundBible, Public Domain.
  Source: https://soundbible.com/1577-Siren-Noise.html
- rise_pulse.wav: "Annoying Alien Buzzer" by Kevan, SoundBible, Public Domain.
  Source: https://soundbible.com/1811-Annoying-Alien-Buzzer.html
- rise_gentle.wav: "Ticking Clock" by KevanGC, SoundBible, Public Domain.
  Source: https://soundbible.com/1580-Ticking-Clock.html

rise_chime.wav is the existing app-generated chime retained as the softer
option.
